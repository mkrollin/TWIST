TWIST, or Teen Women in Science and Technology, is a one-day conference to expose teen women across the state to various career possibilities. TWIST was developed by K-State Salina and the Salina community more than 25 years ago. TWIST is open to any young woman in ninth through 12th grade. Our campus encourages high school sponsors or organizers to register students. Students must register individually to choose their top five career sessions.
